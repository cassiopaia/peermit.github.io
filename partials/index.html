Welcome to PeermitBeta

Peermit.com provides the first of its kind second factor authentication (2FA) service for BitShares to secure your accounts and funds on the blockchain without counterparty-risk. You are always in control!

Peermit requires users to register an account with Peermit and provide us with a reference account name for transaction approvals. Peermit is currently based on the proposal architecture of BitShares.

Note that we are currently running in a beta testing phase and hence recommend to only use small amounts of your money to test this system.
